FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pawn services and policies.

How much can I get for my item?

Loan amounts typically range from 25% to 60% of the item's retail value, depending on the type of item, condition, and current market demand. We provide free appraisals and will explain our valuation process.

What items do you accept for pawn loans?

We accept a wide variety of valuable items including jewelry, electronics, tools, musical instruments, watches, firearms (with proper documentation), and more. Contact us to discuss your specific item.

How long do I have to repay my pawn loan?

Pawn loans in Georgia have a 30-day term with the option to extend for additional 30-day periods. You can pay the interest to extend the loan or pay the full amount to redeem your item at any time.

Do you require a credit check?

No, we never check your credit or report to credit bureaus. Pawn loans are secured by your item, so your credit history doesn't matter. This makes pawn loans accessible to everyone.

What happens if I can't repay my loan?

If you're unable to repay your loan, you can extend it by paying the interest, or the item becomes our property. We always prefer to work with customers to find a solution that works for everyone.

How do I know my items are safe?

All pawned items are stored in our secure, climate-controlled facility with 24/7 monitoring. We're fully licensed and insured, and we maintain detailed records of all transactions for your protection.
