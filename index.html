Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to pawn an item?

Bring a valid government-issued photo ID and the item you wish to pawn. We'll appraise your item and offer you a fair loan amount on the spot.

How long do I have to repay my pawn loan?

Our standard pawn loan term is 30 days, but you can extend your loan by paying the interest. We offer flexible repayment options to fit your needs.

What types of items do you accept?

We accept gold, jewelry, electronics, tools, musical instruments, and more. If you're unsure, just give us a call or stop by!

Is my item safe while it's in pawn?

Absolutely. All pawned items are stored in our secure, monitored facility until you repay your loan and pick them up.
